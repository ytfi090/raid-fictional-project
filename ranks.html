Ranks of R.A.I.D.

Every member of R.A.I.D. serves a purpose. Here is what each rank represents:

🜲 Founder

The supreme leader of R.A.I.D. Sets the vision, approves missions, and oversees all departments. The Founder’s word is final.

✯ High Commander

Second‑in‑command. Directs large operations, coordinates between divisions, and ensures the Founder’s plans are executed.

⚔️ Field Commander

Leads missions in the field. Handles tactical decisions, manages squads, and reports directly to the High Commander.

🛡️ Defense Operative

Specialists in defense and protection. Safeguard members, secure assets, and fortify positions during missions.

🔍 Intelligence Officer

Masters of gathering intel. Track enemy movements, collect useful information, and feed critical data to leadership.

📡 Recruiter

Trusted individuals who scout and recruit new potential members. They evaluate loyalty, skills, and ensure secrecy is maintained.

⚙️ Operatives

The backbone of R.A.I.D. Carry out missions, support commanders, and maintain the reputation of the organization through their actions.

🎖️ Recruits

New members in training. Learn the ways of R.A.I.D., prove loyalty, and work toward promotion into higher ranks.
