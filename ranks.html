📜 Official Ranking System

All ranks of R.A.I.D. explained with their roles and tiers

👁️ Overwatch (Founder – Rehan)

Tier: Command Core
  • Create the vision and missions for R.A.I.D.
  • Approve all major decisions and promotions.
  • Stay hidden within other groups (like C.O.M.P.) to gather intel.
  • Give orders to the Iron Vanguard and oversee every operation.
  • Final authority in disputes and discipline.

🛡️ Iron Vanguard (Second‑in‑Command – Thaahir)

Tier: Command Core
  • Directly commands squads and missions.
  • Enforces the code of R.A.I.D. daily.
  • Takes charge when Overwatch is observing from the shadows.
  • Helps train and guide other members.
  • Reports mission status and problems to Overwatch.

⚔️ Field Marshal (Currently Vacant)

Tier: Command Core
  • Tactical coordinator during missions.
  • Leads large operations involving multiple squads.
  • Oversees Elite Operatives (Shieldbearer, Edge Handler, Eyes, Gatekeeper).

⚡ Shieldbearer (Frontline Lead – Talal)

Tier: Elite Operatives
  • Leads defense and protection during missions.
  • Organizes squads of Night Operatives.
  • Handles conflicts directly when aggression is needed.
  • Trains Initiates in discipline and defense skills.

🔰 Edge Handler (Specialist – Karas)

Tier: Elite Operatives
  • Assigned to special tasks (support, planning, tech, or small missions).
  • Executes missions that require precision but not command.
  • Works closely under orders from the Iron Vanguard or Field Marshal.
  • Keeps a low profile to avoid suspicion.

🧭 Eyes of R.A.I.D. (Intelligence Chief – Mahdi)

Tier: Elite Operatives
  • Gathers information quietly from many sources.
  • Warns R.A.I.D. about threats or changes.
  • Monitors enemy activity (like C.O.M.P.) without being noticed.
  • Shares intel directly with Overwatch and Iron Vanguard.

🤝 Gatekeeper (Recruiter – Zayne Anzil)

Tier: Elite Operatives
  • Watches potential recruits silently.
  • Tests their loyalty with small tasks or situations.
  • Recommends Initiates to Overwatch and Iron Vanguard.
  • Guides Initiates through their first steps and the oath.

✌️ Peacemaker (Currently Vacant)

Tier: Elite Operatives
  • Creates peace between fellow members.
  • Breaks up fights when necessary between the R.A.I.D family.
  • Overlooks all communication sources and creates peace.
  • Keeps important data and assets stored safely for when required.

👮‍♂️ Lawyer (Currently Vacant)

Tier: Elite Operatives
  • Proofreads the articles created by the Overwatch.
  • Spreads rumors to rival teams.
  • Infiltrates enemy bases.
  • Informs the Command Core when important things happen.

🪶 Night Operative (Standard Soldier)

Tier: Core Soldiers
  • Carry out missions assigned by higher ranks.
  • Follow the R.A.I.D. code strictly.
  • Support Shieldbearers, Edge Handlers, and Eyes in their tasks.
  • Train regularly to rise in rank.

🌑 Initiate (Recruit in Training)

Tier: Recruits
  • Learn the R.A.I.D. oath and rules.
  • Prove loyalty through small assignments.
  • Stay under close observation by the Gatekeeper and other ranks.
  • Await promotion to Night Operative once trusted.
