🌿 Duas

“اللّهُمَّ إني أسألُك العفوَ والعافيةَ في الدنيا والآخرة.”

O Allah, I ask You for pardon and well‑being in this world and the Hereafter.

The Prophet ﷺ said: “The best among you are those who learn the Quran and teach it.” (Bukhari)
